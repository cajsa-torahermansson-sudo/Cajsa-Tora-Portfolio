Husqvarna Group

Building a connected garden app — restart, dual track, launch

UX/UI Designer · Global team · Jönköping · Mumbai · Stockholm · 2+ years

First MVP tested poorly. Instead of patching, we restarted. Introduced Dual Track agile, embedded with the dev team in Jönköping every two weeks — the first designer to do so. Team grew from 6 to 15. Second usability test: strong across all core flows.

Method
Dual Track agile — discovery and delivery running in parallel across 3-week sprints
Validation
2 rounds usability testing (8 users each) — major recovery from round 1 to round 2

Skatteverket

Making open government APIs findable and useful

UX/UI Designer · Scrum Master · Digitala samarbeten · SAFe · Remote (COVID-19)

Low traffic on the open data and API service. Root causes: poor navigation, sparse content, no clear developer entry point. In-depth interviews across decision-makers, strategists, and developers. Distilled three core user stories that drove the entire design direction.

Research
Interviews with companies using Skatteverket APIs — C-suite to developers
Validation
Internal technical test + quantitative survey to ~30 companies — MVP confirmed ready
Learning
To get 20 responses you need 100+ contacts. Documented as team learning.
